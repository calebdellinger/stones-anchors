Integrity Makes Leadership Credible

Leadership gains influence through trust, and trust is built through character.
Leadership principle
Men will not fully follow a leader whose private life and public words do not agree. Integrity means doing what is right even when it is inconvenient, expensive, or invisible to everyone else. It keeps authority from becoming manipulation.
Verses and takeaway
Proverbs 10:9
Whoever walks in integrity walks securely.
1 Timothy 3:2
An overseer must be above reproach.
Titus 2:7–8
Show yourself in all respects to be a model of good works, with integrity and dignity.
A leader's word becomes weighty when others know he pays the same price in secret that he asks from others in public.
