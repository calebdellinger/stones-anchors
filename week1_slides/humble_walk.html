A Humble Walk Stays Teachable

Humility becomes visible in how a man walks, talks, and treats people he could easily overlook.
Leadership posture
A humble man is approachable. He is not puffed up, and he is not collapsed into false weakness. He stays teachable, grounded, and near to other people.
Verses
Micah 6:8
Do justice, love mercy, and walk humbly with your God.
Philippians 2:5
Have this mind among yourselves, which is yours in Christ Jesus.
James 1:21
Receive with meekness the implanted word.
What this looks like

Justice — ethics, fairness, and right dealing.

Mercy — empathy, second chances, and support for others.

Humility — low ego, teachability, and refusing to look down on people.
