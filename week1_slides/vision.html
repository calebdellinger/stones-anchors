Vision Sees the Build Before the Start

A leader sees the finished build before the first shovel hits the dirt.

Vision gives the team a destination, a reason for the work, and a way to measure whether the build is on course. Without it, men become reactive, scattered, and easy to discourage.

Leadership implication
Strategic thinking is not abstract dreaming. It is the discipline of seeing where the work must go, anticipating obstacles before they arrive, and communicating the path clearly enough that others can move with confidence.

Proverbs 29:18
Where there is no vision, the people perish.

Hebrews 11:1
Faith is the assurance of things hoped for and the conviction of things not seen.

Philippians 3:13–14
Forgetting what lies behind, press on toward the goal.
