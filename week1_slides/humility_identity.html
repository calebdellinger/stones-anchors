Humility Knows Who You Are

Humility is not thinking less of yourself. It is seeing yourself accurately before God.

A humble man knows both sides of the truth: he has a calling, and he is not self-sustaining. He can stand confidently in his assignment while also knowing that without Christ he has no life, breath, or lasting strength.

Leadership principle
Humility keeps identity from drifting into pride and keeps dependence from collapsing into insecurity. It is strong because it is honest about both gift and weakness.
Philippians 2:3
Do nothing from selfish ambition, but in humility count others more significant than yourselves.
Romans 12:3
Think with sober judgment, according to the measure of faith God has assigned.
1 Corinthians 15:10
By the grace of God I am what I am.
The humble leader knows who he is, but he also knows who he is not.
