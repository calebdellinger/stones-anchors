Week 3 · Love

Sacrifice

"Whoever wants to save their life will lose it, but whoever loses their life for me will find it." — Matthew 16:25

Anchored in Luke 10 (The Good Samaritan) and Ephesians 5, this week examines the practical cost of sacrificial love — what it means to lay down your life as a disciple, a husband, a father, and a leader. Supporting passages from Mark 10:45, Romans 12:1, and John 15:13.
